A History of Rome in Maps

An overview of Rome through the ages

The Roman Empire, a dominating force of the Mediterranean and Europe. The Roman civilization dominated the western world culturally, economically, and militarily for 2214 years. The legacy that Rome left behind changed our world. The Roman legacy was the driving force for the renaissance, the spread of Christianity, our modern legal codes, the worlds most spoken languages, among countless others. In that time, the foundation of world as we know it today was laid.
